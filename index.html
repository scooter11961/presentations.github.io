Presentations by Scott Hatch

  1. Who owns Quality anyhow?

    "The definition of Quality Assurance has been changing rapidly in the software industry. In a few short years, we have gone from a model where requirements, and testing procedures where created months in advance, and waterfall release cycles created a back-and-forth bug fix cycle between development, and testing. In today's fast paced software industry, Agile teams are demanding more of us in terms of delivery, speed to market, and of course, still quality! This is a talk about what some teams have found useful to perform at this new level."

    - AOL / MapQuest Brown Bag in Dublin, June 2013
